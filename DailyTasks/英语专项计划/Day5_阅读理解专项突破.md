**Passage 1: The Benefits of Reading**

Reading is one of the most important skills in our life. It not only helps us learn new knowledge, but also improves our thinking ability.

First, reading can increase our vocabulary. When we read books, we meet many new words. We can guess their meanings from the context or look them up in the dictionary. The more we read, the more words we know.

Second, reading helps us understand the world better. Books can take us to different places and times. We can learn about history, science, culture, and many other things through reading.

Third, reading is good for our mental health. When we read, we can relax and forget our worries. Reading interesting stories can make us happy.

In conclusion, reading brings us many benefits. We should develop a good reading habit from now on.

**Passage 2: A Lesson from Nature**

Jadav Payeng is a man from India. When he was a teenager, he saw many animals die because floods had washed away the forest. He felt very sad and decided to do something.

Every day, Jadav planted trees on a sandbar near his village. At first, people laughed at him. They thought one person couldn't make a difference. But Jadav didn't give up. He planted trees day after day, year after year.

Thirty years later, his hard work paid off. The sandbar became a beautiful forest with many trees and animals. The forest is now home to tigers, elephants, and many birds. It's bigger than Central Park in New York!

Jadav's story teaches us an important lesson: one person CAN make a difference. If we believe in ourselves and never give up, we can achieve great things.

**Passage 3: AI in Healthcare**

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is changing many parts of our lives, including healthcare. AI can help doctors in many ways.

First, AI can analyze medical images quickly and accurately. For example, AI can look at X-rays and find diseases that doctors might miss. This helps doctors make better diagnoses.

Second, AI can predict diseases. By analyzing patients' data, AI can tell who might get certain diseases in the future. This allows doctors to prevent illnesses before they happen.

Third, AI can help create personalized treatment plans. Every patient is different, and AI can find the best treatment for each person.

However, AI also has limitations. It cannot replace human doctors because it lacks empathy and human judgment. The best solution is to combine AI technology with human expertise.
